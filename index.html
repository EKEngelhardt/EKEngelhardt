Eva Katharina Engelhardt

Doctoral Researcher

Biochange Lab

Technical University of Munich

Currently doctoral researcher wrapping up my thesis at Biochange Lab in Freising at the Chair for Terrestrial Ecology of the Technical University of Munich. I am interested in macroecology, nature conservation, biodiversity monitoring and modelling, with focus on animals, especially insects and birds. See also my updated short CV.
